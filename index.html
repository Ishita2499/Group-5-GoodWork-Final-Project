What We Are About


GoodWork is Canada's largest community of environmental changemakers, sustainability professionals, job seekers and volunteers. It's a hub for all Canadians working towards a green, healthy, sustainable world. Our mission is to connect motivated, passionate people with opportunities in environment and sustainability — meaningful work with environmental organizations, sustainable business, municipal and federal government.
